# Ironworks Gym — Forge Your Best Self

A modern, responsive landing page for **Ironworks Gym**, a premium training facility based in Millbrook. This project showcases the gym's identity, training programs, membership plans, and community-driven philosophy.

![Ironworks Gym Preview](https://via.placeholder.com/1200x630?text=Ironworks+Gym+Preview)

## 🏋️ Features

- **Fully responsive** – Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- **Mobile-first navigation** – Hamburger menu for small screens.
- **Smooth scroll** – Anchor links with offset for fixed header.
- **Scroll reveal animations** – Elements fade in as you scroll.
- **Modern design** – Dark theme with bold typography and industrial accents.
- **Accessibility** – Semantic HTML, ARIA labels, keyboard navigation support.
- **Performance optimized** – External CSS/JS, minimal dependencies, no external images.
