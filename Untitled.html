Our Story

BUILT
HERE.

Ironworks was born in Millbrook in 2008 with one squat rack and a borrowed stereo. Today we're a 12,000 sq ft facility built by — and for — this community.

We're not a franchise. We don't have a corporate office. Every decision we make is rooted in what's best for the people who walk through our doors every day — neighbors, parents, athletes, and beginners alike.

Our coaches live here. Our members are our friends. That's what makes Ironworks different.
